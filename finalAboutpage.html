our history


The idea of ZenithDevs began as a project for Platforms Based Development: Web by Jay R. Cañezo and Gian Joebert B. Caparas. What started as a classroom collaboration soon grew into a shared vision to provide innovative software and IT solutions.

Initially focused on small web applications and automation tools for local businesses, ZenithDevs steadily expanded its expertise to mobile development, cloud integration, IT consulting, and enterprise systems.

From its academic roots to becoming a trusted partner in digital transformation, ZenithDevs continues to grow—driven by innovation, reliability, and a passion for crafting excellent solutions.



Founded: Febuary 18, 2025.
